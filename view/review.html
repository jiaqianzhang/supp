Beautiful lake in Slovenia - The_Travel_Family on July 27, 2024

Another beautiful lake in Slovenia. Some tips watch out for parking as some only give maximum 2 hours. Get a boat across the lake and hike back. Do this early as all the small private beach spots along the lake get taken. Take a dip, it's very refreshing. Enjoy.

Amazing caves - PacoDaTurtle on August 1, 2024

Amazing caves, really big The Zoo is not worth a visit unless you are really into cave reptiles and bugs. 4-5 restaurants to eat at. Remedier some Cash for parring... around 6 euros.

Great short break to Bled & Ljubljana - Howard M on July 30, 2024

Absolutely fabulous place. Can walk around the lake, can do summer toboggan ride, zip wiring and nearby is the Vintgar Gorge which is very a well organised walk above and alongside the white water rapids. Need to be fit to get back although they run a shuttle service. Nice restaurants in Bled too.
